# Depth Psychology Differentiation

## What PCA Actually Is

Psycho-Contextual Analysis is a proprietary clinical framework developed over 20 years of practice by Mathew Quaschnick, LPCC. It is the analytical engine underneath iVASA's voice conversations.

PCA's core function: **translating what you say into what you mean** — surfacing the patterns underneath language that the speaker cannot see alone.

## What "Depth" Actually Means in Posts

Never use the word "depth" alone — it requires grounding. Depth means:
- Finding the pattern underneath the pattern
- The thing you keep doing in different relationships with different people
- What you actually mean when you say "I'm fine"
- The connection between the thing that happened at 8 and the thing you keep doing at 38

## The Key Phrase

**"We translate what you say into what you mean."**
