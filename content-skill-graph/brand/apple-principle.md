# The Apple Principle: Lead with WHY

## The Rule

Never lead with WHAT we do or HOW we do it. Always lead with WHY it matters.

## The Three Layers

**WHY** — The human problem. The reason we exist. The thing that keeps people up at night.
> "158 million people cannot get a therapy appointment."

**HOW** — Our approach. What makes us different.
> "We translate what you say into what you mean."

**WHAT** — The product. The feature. The thing.
> "iVASA. Voice therapy at depth psychology levels."

## The Order

Every piece of content, every post, every sentence — WHY first. Always.

## Applied to X Posts

The post should not mention iVASA at all in Phase 1. We are building problem awareness. The WHY is the entire post. iVASA lives in the bio and the pinned post — not in every piece of content.

**Wrong order (WHAT first):**
"iVASA uses AI to provide therapy-level support to people who can't see a therapist."

**Right order (WHY first):**
"She drove 3 hours each way for her appointment. It was the only therapist within range taking new patients. She was lucky."

## The Test

Cover the last sentence of your post. Does the rest of it stand alone as a human truth worth caring about? If yes — the WHY is working.
