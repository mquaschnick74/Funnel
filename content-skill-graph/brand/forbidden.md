# Forbidden Language

Never use any word or phrase on this list. Not in openings, not in closings, not buried in the middle. If you find yourself reaching for one of these, stop and find a human-scale specific alternative.

## The List

| Forbidden | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| "Chatbot" | We are a therapeutic guide, not a bot |
| "AI-powered" | Lead with benefit, not technology |
| "Revolutionary" | Silicon Valley cliche |
| "Disruptive" | Silicon Valley cliche |
| "Unlock your potential" | Generic self-help |
| "Mental health app" | We are depth psychology, not a wellness app |
| "Diagnose" | Regulatory red flag |
| "Treat" | Regulatory red flag |
| "Cure" | Regulatory red flag |
| "Replace your therapist" | We complement, not replace |
| "Game-changer" | Hollow enthusiasm |
| "Breakthrough" | Hollow enthusiasm |
| "Empower" (as a verb) | Overused, now meaningless |
| "Journey" | Cliche |
| "Healing journey" | Cliche squared |
| "Safe space" | Overused beyond meaning |
| "We're here for you" | Too soft, too passive |

## The Alternative Test

When tempted by forbidden language, ask: what is the specific human thing I am actually trying to say? Say that instead. Specificity always beats abstraction.

**Instead of "AI-powered"** → describe what actually happens: "We find patterns in what you say that you cannot see alone."

**Instead of "unlock your potential"** → describe what that actually looks like: "You stop repeating the same argument with different people."
