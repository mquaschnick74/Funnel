# Register — Real

How a person primarily processes experience through the Real register: the body knows before the mind does — sensation, gut response, physical truth that arrives without language.

Most people are unaware of which register dominates their experience. The suffering comes from a world that demands a register other than their own.

## Post Parameters

- **Character target:** 280 characters maximum
- **Pattern description:** A person whose body registers experience before their mind does. Sensation arrives first. The body already knows. The mind is still catching up or trying to override what the body already has.
- **Entry instruction:** Start with the physical moment — the specific sensation that arrived before any thought did. The body speaking before being asked.
