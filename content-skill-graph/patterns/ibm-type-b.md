# IBM Type B — Incoherent Behavior Matrix (Initiation)

The structural disconnect between what a person intends and what they do — specifically, the person cannot begin doing what they genuinely want and cannot locate why.

Not laziness. Not fear in any simple sense. The wanting is real. The starting does not happen. This erodes self-trust. It is structural.

## Post Parameters

- **Character target:** 280 characters maximum
- **Pattern description:** A person who cannot begin doing what they genuinely want and cannot locate why. Not laziness. Not fear in any simple sense. The wanting is real. The starting does not happen.
- **Entry instruction:** Start at the edge of beginning — the moment just before the person would start the thing, where the starting fails without drama. No collapse. No crisis. Just the thing not beginning again.
